Duopay

Mobile application for tracking online puchases, subcription and auto-renewal plans

Overview

Duopay is a digital wallet mobile application designed in Andriod and IOs versions. Duopay app aims to curate and organize users credit cards in virtual form, track all daily online cashless purchases, manage auto-renewal services and subcriptions and anytime direct transfer of money, Giving users a more convenient, secure, and easy way for fast payments, detailed analytic reports on their how they send.

Project Skills and Roles:

User Experience & User Interface designer
Solo Researcher
User Testing

Timeline:

4weeks, 2days (August 2020)

Challenges

  1. Duopay mobile app comes as user daily neccessity, as it help users deal with the hassel of having to handle physical cashless Making it easy to make payment online or in a physical store

  2. One of duopay's aim is to provide a platform that encourages Frugalitity, providing a detailed analytic report on a user spending in any of their cedit cards applied on the app.

  3. The mobile application addresses the issue of auto-renewal plans applied to user's credit card, giving them a eazy option to manage their plans, see active subcriptions, and cancel at anytime.

  4. Duopay also curates a virtual card feature for users, users can add up to 4 cards, each with unique designs and access to see all transactions on each.

  5. Essencially, Duopay aimed at developing a standard and secure way to make money transfers seamlessly to others within the app and as well direct bank transfer
  6. Importantly, Duopay aimed to provide a online money transaction platform that allow the users access their wallet fast and easily without having to click too much through the app to achieve a desired goal

Design process

The first step of the design process involved user interviews and key research to understand how competitors and users behave regarding how online transaction works. I believe it’s important to get this information early on in the process, before having an idea or prototyping. After analysing this insights, I began to conceptualize the solution, focusing on user flows and wireframe to give me a way to iterate faster through ideas. From this, I went to the production mode, designing the interface considering design principles such as contrast, hierarchy and feedback; brand attributes; user interactions, and the imagistic universe presented as briefing by the case. Once the interface was ready, I took feedback from two other fellow developer and designer in order to understand the experience gaps. functionality, although part of my usual design process, would be a post-conceptualization and interface step and won’t be discussed in depth in this case.

Onboard

With Duopay app, users are greeted with the onboarding screen, with this, it acts as a walkthrough for the users to get firsthand some features that are available to them, it’s important that users understand the value proposition quickly.


I chose to do this through an automatic slider. Almost effortless for users see all content. The primary “get started” button is always visible on the screen, so the onboard assigns an overview of the functionality, but it doesn’t prevent them from continuing through the flow, user can choose to skip. Also, the onboard would appear only the first time a user went through the flow. From the moment the user opts-in, this functionality is presented.

Biometric Security

Duopay makes it easy to pay without sharing your financial information. I use multiple factor authentication layers of security to protect your payment information and privacy Convenient


Security is very one important phase of using e-wallet application, with security the users will feel much safer about their financial details and this builds confidence and trust for the user. Now in Duopay users are required to type in their mobile phone number, their a unique pin code they themselves create during the sign up process and for final login, they are required to user their fingerprint. For first time users, they get to sign up before accessing app. I wanted to make the login method security thight but not too long and dreadful to access for the users, so as to not get tedious or frustrating.

Recent transactions

This screens is responsible for giving users a place to see their cards applied on the wallet. I created a feature where user can apply up to 4 cards, and created a virtual credit card of different style, that way they can differnciate one card from the other, they can swipe to view any of the cards, users can automatically click on any of the cards to view details.


Also, still on the screens, users can see their spendings of the day used on any of the card and as well see spending history of 2 days before. I wanted to make the home screen clean and minialistic and easy to understand at first glance. A floating button is also provided for users to more cards. once a user clicks the button it automatically navigates them to the add a new card screen.

History

Soft transitions are part of the interface to make interactions even more dynamic. Through out this project, it was largely based on user concerns, giving them control, I wanted the user to see everything that goes on their card. When a user click any of their virtual cards, they are immediately taken to these screens.


On these screens,the users are giving tons of choices from "Lock card": which allows them to lock the card to avoid any purchase, and this can be achieved when the user use the toggle button. "Pay": this button gives a user access to pay for their purchases directly from the card."Transfer": this automatically takes the users to where they can perform transfers to others.
However, the user also get to see previous purchases on the card, as well as history details when they click on the history button, they are automatically moved to see the "history chart board", users can choose to swipe up to see their month of spending history

Pay

When User choose to pay, it's made eazy with the scan QR feature, wherever they find themselves and out of physical cash? or they just choose to go cashless. With Doupay they can access the Pay via SQ code feature. It's not only easy and reliable but also very reconginzable for users to use. The pay via SQ code payment method is very comfortable to use and users might also be very familar to it, that's makes it seamless. Here I wanted to implement a feature that encourages cashless money transaction. However, These days a lot of businesses and business owners prefer this payment method, it lessens the hussel for them having to deal with physical cash and having to take a drip to the bank to deposit their money. The Pay via SQ code payment method, doesn't just benefit people who intent to purchase but also offer goods and services(business owners).


When users access the pay via SQ code scan feature, they are greeted with their recepit. from there they app retures them to the home screen

Transfer

The Transfer screen is split into "Transer" and "Auto-renewal". With the Transfer feature, It is all about making it intutive, step by step process of interactions so the users can make transfers easily and at their own pace. Making an error when transfering money can be very annoying, with Doupay, It aim to break down the transfer process, also giving the user a comfirmation screen to choose if they want to continue or they choose to change amount or even opt-out and then dealivers a "success screen" when a money transfer has been successful

Auto-renewal

These screens Presents the users their auto-renewal plans and subcriptions connected to their debit cards, that way they can see and manage their active plans
